## Pehle kyun fail hua — honestly

Spreadsheet: Ek ne banayi, doosre ne kabhi khola nahi.

Shared notes app: Good intent, zero consistency.

Budgeting app with features: Bank link karo, categories set karo, goals add karo — done karne se pehle hi thak gaye.

Yeh laziness nahi hai. Yeh **friction ka problem** hai. Jab karna mushkil ho, log karte nahi.

---

## Couple tracking ke 3 real problems

### Problem 1: Entry sirf ek hi karta hai

"Main track karta hoon" — ek insaan pe saara load. Doosra kabhi involved nahi hota. Ek din woh bhi thak jaata hai — system crash.

### Problem 2: "Baad mein enter karenge"

Aaj chai pi, kal enter karenge — kal aata hi nahi. 3 din baad yaad hi nahi kitna hua.

### Problem 3: Koi shared view nahi

Dono alag-alag track kar rahe hain, combine karke kabhi nahi dekhte. Toh saath tracking ka fayda kya?

---

## Jo actually kaam karta hai: 3 rules

### Rule 1: Real-time log karo — dono

Jab kharcha ho, tab log karo. Baad mein nahi.

> `sabzi 340` — Neha ne log kiya, grocery se nikalte hue

> `petrol 800` — Vikas ne log kiya, pump pe

Dono apna-apna log karte hain. Shared view mein sab dikh jaata hai automatically.

### Rule 2: Categories simple rakho

5-6 se zyada mat banao:

- Grocery
- Eating out
- Transport
- Bills & EMI
- Health
- Personal

Har cheez yahan fit hoti hai. Sub-categories mein mat jao — woh system ko heavy karta hai.

### Rule 3: Weekly review — 10 minute, no more

Hafte mein ek baar — Sunday raat ya Monday subah — dono milke dekhte hain:

- Kitna hua kul?
- Kahan zyada gaya?
- Agla hafte kya ek cheez adjust karein?

Sirf ek adjustment. Overwhelming mat karo.

---

## Kya track karein — aur kya chhodein

**Track karo:**
- Grocery aur daily kharche — har baar, turant
- Bills, EMI, subscriptions — jis din bharo
- Baahar khana, outings — entry turant karo
- Kisi ko diya ya kisi se lena — note karo

**Mat obsess karo:**
- Har ₹10 pe — reasonable spending pe guilt ki zaroorat nahi
- Exact category — agar yaad nahi, "Misc" likho aur aage badho
- Perfect numbers — approximate bhi kaam chalaata hai

---

## Monthly review mein kya dekhein

Mahine ke end mein sirf 20 minute:

1. **Combined total** — dono ka milake kitna hua
2. **Top 3 categories** — kahan gaya zyada paisa
3. **Unplanned kharche** — jo plan mein nahi tha
4. **Agla mahine ka sirf ek adjustment** — ek cheez badlo, sab nahi

Yeh sab dikhta rehega toh saal ke end mein clearly pata chalega savings kyun nahi hui — ya kyun hui.

---

## Ek practical week kaise shuru karein

**Day 1:** Dono ek-ek kharcha log karo. Kuch bhi — chai, auto, grocery.

**Day 2-6:** Jab bhi kharcha ho — log karo. Miss hua? Koi baat nahi, next.

**Day 7:** 10 minute mein saath mein dekho — kul kitna hua, kahan gaya.

Pehle hafte mein sirf consistency chahiye. Accuracy baad mein aati hai.
