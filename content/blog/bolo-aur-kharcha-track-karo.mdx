## Typing se ajeeb lagta hai na?

Ek cafe mein baitho, coffee piyo, ₹60 diye. Ab phone nikaalo, app kholo, title type karo ("Coffee"), amount type karo (60), category dhundo (Food), date check karo, save karo. Tab tak toh next customer aa gaya.

Yehi problem hai. Expense aata hai, time nahi.

## Voice-first expense tracking kya hai?

Voice-first expense tracking mehsool karte hain bas bolke. Aap mic tap karke bolo:

> "Coffee 6 bucks"
> "Uber 17"
> "Groceries 42"
> "Netflix 15 a month"
> "auto 80"
> "chai 20 rupees"
> "dinner with friends 150"

AI khud samajh jayega:
- Amount: $6, $17, $42, $15
- Category: Coffee, Transport, Groceries, Bills
- Date: aaj

Koi form nahi, koi typing nahi. Sirf 3 second mein done.
