## Typing se thak nahi gaye?

Ek cafe mein baitho, coffee piyo, ₹60 diya. Ab phone nikaalo, app kholo, title type karo ("Coffee"), amount type karo (60), category dhundo (Food), date check karo, save karo. Tab tak toh next customer aa gaya.

Yehi problem hai. Expense aata hai, time nahi.

## Voice-first expense tracking kya hai?

Voice-first mein sirf bolna hai — bas. Mic tap karo aur bolo:

> "Coffee ₹60"
> "Uber ₹180"
> "Groceries ₹450"
> "Netflix iss mahine ka"
> "auto ₹80"
> "chai ₹20"
> "dinner with friends ₹150"

AI khud samajh jayega:
- Amount: ₹60, ₹180, ₹450, ₹649
- Category: Food, Transport, Groceries, Bills
- Date: aaj

Koi form nahi, koi typing nahi. Sirf 3 second mein done.

## Features

### AI samajhdaar hai

AI messy phrases ko perfect categories mein badal deta hai:

- "Grocery ₹78" → Groceries, ₹78
- "lunch with Sam ₹40" → Food, ₹40
- "Netflix this month" → Bills, ₹649

Title, amount, category — sab khud nikal jaate hain.

### Lock screen widgets

Phone unlock kiye bina expense dekh sakte ho. Lock screen pe widget, dynamic island — total immediately visible.

### 3 second mein done

Traditional apps: 32 seconds, 22 taps.
Voice-first: 3 second, bolke done.

Forms, dropdowns, account pickers — sab gaye.

### Auto breakdown

Har hafte, har mahine — kharche ka breakdown khud dikhta rehta hai. Kitna gaya, kahan gaya — sab saaf.

### Offline bhi kaam karta hai

Subway mein, flight mein, kahin bhi signal na ho — no problem. Connection aate hi sab sync ho jaayega.

### Privacy first

Voice on-device rehta hai. Kuch sold nahi hota, leak nahi hota, kisi aur model mein train nahi hota.

### Galat ho gaya? Undo karo

AI ki parsing galat hui? Koi baat nahi. Undo tap karo, woh entry wapis chali jaayegi. Firse bolo sahi se.
