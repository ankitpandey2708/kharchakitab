## Sirf ek app, kai alag zindagiyan

KharchaKitab koi ek type ke insaan ke liye nahi bana. Ghar chalane waali bhi use karti hai, auto driver bhi, college student bhi. Har koi apne hisaab se likhta hai — kharcha, kaam, reminder, jo bhi urgent lage.

Yahan hain kuch asli log aur woh jis tarah se KharchaKitab unke kaam aata hai.

---

## Reena — housewife, Mumbai

Ghar ka hisaab rakhna matlab rozana 10-15 choti-choti cheezein track karna. Sabzi, school fees, family ki health — sab ek jagah.

> `sabzi 120`
> `milk 80`
> `auto 60`

---

## Raju Bhai — auto driver, Pune

Subah fuel, din mein kamaai, shaam ko tiffin — har cheez count hoti hai. Saath mein yaar-doston ke chote-chote hisaab aur EMI ka reminder jo kabhi miss nahi hona chahiye.

> `petrol 400`
> `kira aaj 1350`
> `chai 20`
> `lunch 90`
> `collect from suresh 500`
> `EMI 3000 on 5th`

Raju Bhai zyada nahi likhte — but jo likhte hain, woh sach mein kaam ka hota hai.

## Arjun — college student, Hyderabad

Pocket money kab khatam ho jaata hai pata hi nahi chalta. Canteen, photocopy, chai — sab chhota lagta hai, milake bada ho jaata hai. Deadlines alag tension.

> `canteen 65`
> `2 samosa @ 15`
> `photocopy 30`

---

## Sunita Ma'am — school teacher, Jaipur

Salary mahine mein ek baar, kharche roz. Class ke liye samaan, submissions, routine — sab track karna padta hai taaki koi cheez chhoote nahi.

> `red pens 12 @ 8`
> `chart paper 5 @ 12`

---

## Mangesh — factory worker, Nashik

Hafte ki tanjkha, roz ka aana-jaana, overtime — aur kahin-kahin kisi ne kuch liya to woh bhi yaad rakhna. Zyada nahi likhte, par jo likhte hain woh pakka yaad rehna chahiye.

> `bus 40`
> `tiffin 60`
> `overtime 420`

---

## Sharma Ji — kirana owner, Delhi

Kirana mein cash fast chalti hai. Aaj kitna aaya, Gupta ne kab dena hai, atta-tel kab mangwana hai — sab ek jagah nahi raha toh hisaab bigad jaata hai.

> `aaj ki cash 4200`
> `collect from gupta 650`
> `rent 8000 on 1st`

---

## Priya — freelance designer, Bangalore

Income kabhi zyada, kabhi kam. Deadlines, client follow-ups, invoices — sab ka hisaab rakhna padta hai. Bhool gaye toh paisa nahi aata.

> `figma subscription 800`
> `invoice acme corp 35000`
