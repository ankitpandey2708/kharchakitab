## Sabse common couple argument jo paisa shuru karta hai

"Ek joint account banate hain."
"Nahi, mujhe apni independence chahiye."
"Toh split karein?"
"Par ghar ka kharcha equal nahi hoga."

Har couple yahan aake ruk jaata hai.

---

## 4 models hain — honestly

### 1. Fully joint — sab ek jagah

Dono ki salary ek account mein. Saara kharcha wahaan se.

**Kab kaam karta hai:** Similar income. High trust. Same financial goals.

**Problem:** Ek ki personal spending doosre ko dikh rahi hai — chai se lekar shoes tak. Privacy zero.

---

### 2. Fully split — sab alag-alag

Har koi apna kharcha khud uthata hai. Shared bills divide hoti hain.

**Kab kaam karta hai:** Early relationships. Different incomes. Strong personal financial habits.

**Problem:** Ghar ka kharcha accurately split karna mushkil hai. Koi ek baar zyada deta hai, track karna bhool jaata hai — aur phir argument.

---

### 3. Income-based proportional split

Jo zyada kamata hai, woh zyada contribute karta hai. Raj ₹80K kamata hai, Priya ₹40K — toh 2:1 ratio mein shared expenses.

**Kab kaam karta hai:** Unequal incomes. Dono ko fair feel hona chahiye.

**Problem:** Calculation complex hai. Agar income change ho — salary hike, job switch — toh har baar revisit karna padta hai.

---

### 4. Hybrid — shared pool + personal accounts

Ek joint account sirf ghar ke kharche ke liye. Baaki personal accounts alag rehte hain.

**Kab kaam karta hai:** Most couples ke liye best fit. Independence bhi, transparency bhi.

**Problem:** Shared account mein kitna daalna hai — yeh decide karna padta hai. Aur jo shared pool se jaata hai woh track karna padta hai.

---

## 2026 mein actually kya kaam karta hai?

Honest answer: **model matter nahi karta. Tracking matter karta hai.**

Fully joint couple bhi fail ho sakta hai agar koi nahi dekh raha kahan ja raha hai paisa.

Fully split couple bhi smooth chal sakta hai agar dono apna-apna log karte hain aur shared bills clearly divide hain.

> Jo bhi system choose karo — uske andar visibility raho.

---

## Common mistakes — model se zyada important

❌ "Baad mein track karenge" — baad mein yaad nahi rehta

❌ Ek insaan pe saara financial load — burnout aata hai, resentment aata hai

❌ Assumptions — "tune bhara hoga" — bills miss hoti hain

❌ Month-end mein pehli baar account dekhna — tab tak kuch nahi badal sakta

---

## Toh kya karein abhi?

1. Model choose karo — koi bhi, jo dono ko fair lage
2. Shared expenses clearly decide karo (rent, grocery, bills, EMIs)
3. Dono real-time log karein — jab kharcha ho, tab entry karo
4. Hafte mein ek baar 10 minute review — saath mein

System perfect nahi hona chahiye. Consistent hona chahiye.
