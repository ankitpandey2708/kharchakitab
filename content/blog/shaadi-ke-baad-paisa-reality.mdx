## Honeymoon khatam. Reality shuru.

Flight land ki. Ghar aaye. Teen din mein pehle bills aaye.

Electricity: ₹2,400.
Internet: ₹999.
Society maintenance: ₹1,800.

Aur abhi toh grocery bhi nahi khareeddi thi.

Yahi hota hai — shaadi ke baad pehle mahine mein.

---

## Expectation 1: "Dono milke manage kar lenge"

**Reality:** Koi decide nahi karta kaun manage karega.

Dono assume karte hain doosra dekh raha hai. Bills late hoti hain. Account ka balance surprise karta hai.

Grocery kaun khareedata hai? EMI kaun track karta hai? Yeh baat actually karni padti hai — honeymoon ke baad pehle hafte mein hi.

---

## Expectation 2: "Dono kamate hain, toh paisa hoga"

**Reality:** Do alag spending patterns, ek hi ghar.

Ek chai pe ₹30 nahi deta. Doosra Amazon pe bina soche order karta hai. Ek dining out prefer karta hai. Doosra ghar pe khana banana chahta hai.

Combined income nahi — **combined habits** matter karti hain. Aur woh tab tak nahi dikh tein jab tak track na karo.

---

## Expectation 3: "App se ho jaayega"

**Reality:** Week 2 mein abandon.

Shaadi ke baad ek budgeting app download kiya. Features bahut the. Bank link karo, categories set karo, goals add karo, reports dekho.

Ek hafte mein ek thak gaya. Doosre hafte mein koi use nahi kar raha tha.

Zyada features = zyada friction = system fail hota hai.

---

## Expectation 4: "Pehle settle ho jaayein, phir plan karenge"

**Reality:** "Settle" kabhi nahi hota.

Pehle mahine mein shifting ka kharcha. Doosre mein furniture. Teesre mein koi function. Chauthey mein kuch aur.

Jab tum plan ka wait karte ho — paisa already ja chuka hota hai. Real-time tracking hi seedha raasta hai, planning ke baad nahi.

---

## Expectation 5: "Bade kharche hote hain, aate-jaate rehte hain"

**Reality:** Ek-do bade kharche saara calculation bigad dete hain.

Car service: ₹12,000. Relative ki shaadi gift: ₹5,000. Medical emergency: ₹8,000.

Yeh "aata-jaata" nahi — yeh unplanned drain hai. Bina tracking ke yeh invisible rehta hai, aur suddenly account empty lagta hai.

---

## Jo actually kaam karta hai

Complicated system nahi chahiye. Sirf yeh:

**Jab kharcha ho, tab log karo.** Dono. Real-time.

> `electricity 2400` — jis din bhara, usi din entry

> `grocery 1650` — market se nikalte hue, 3 second mein

> `car service 12000` — service station pe baithe hue, done

Hafte mein ek baar saath mein dekhte hain. 10 minute. Ek cheez adjust karte hain.

Boring hai? Haan. Kaam karta hai? Bilkul.

---

## Pehle mahine mein ek cheez karo

Koi perfect system plan mat karo abhi.

Aaj ek kharcha log karo. Kal doosra. Is hafte ka combined total dekho saath mein.

Yahi starting point hai — aur yahi kaafi hai.
