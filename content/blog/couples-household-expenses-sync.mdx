## Shaadi ya live-in — paisa toh dono ka hai

Raat ke 10 baj rahe hain. Aap grocery ka bill dekh rahe ho — ₹2,400. Partner poochh raha hai: "aaj kal kahan ja raha hai itna paisa?"

Jawab hai: dono ke accounts mein. Par kisi ko exact idea nahi.

Ek ne petrol bhara, doosre ne sabzi li. Ek ne OTT ka subscription renewl kiya, doosre ne bacche ki school fees bhari. Mahine ke end mein — sirf confusion aur thodi si behas.

Yeh sirf aapke ghar ki baat nahi hai.

---

## Problem kya hai actually?

Jab dono log kharcha karte hain aur track koi nahi karta, toh yehi hota hai:

- "Maine socha tune dekh liya hoga" — doosre ne bhi yahi socha
- Month end mein account low, reason unknown
- EMI ki date yaad nahi, koi reminder set nahi kiya
- "Tune itna kyun liya?" — uncomfortable conversation

Spreadsheet try kiya. Shared notes try ki. Phir bhi kuch na kuch chhoot jaata hai.

## Kya farak padta hai practically?

**EMI aur bills double-entry nahi hoti.** Ek ne log kiya, doosra dekh sakta hai — dobara log karne ki zaroorat nahi.

**Grocery ka hisaab ek jagah.** Koi paise dono side se nahi ja rahe bina reason ke.

**Travel aur outings track hote hain.** "Aaj ₹1,200 dinner pe gaye" — clear entry, koi guessing nahi.

**Month-end surprise nahi.** Week-by-week spending dikh rahi hai, toh end-of-month shock nahi aata.
